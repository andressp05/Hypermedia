FAQ

1: What is Paper House?
Answer: Paper House is a educational simulation of a bookshop created for the subject Hypermedia Applications of Politecnico di Milano university.
2: How can I filter the books?
Answer: You can filter them by literary genre and by topic. Also you can group them by literary genre and by topic. And in addition, we have a list of favourites and of bestsellers that you can see as well.

6: Can I buy a book?
Answer: No, our website simulates an e-commerce library in which you can simulate that you are buying a book by making a reservation of it.
7: Should I be registered for make a reservation of a book?
Answer: Yes. You can create your account totally free.
8: Do you have a physical shop?
Answer: No, we are an online e-commerce bookshop but we have done the website in Milano, Italy. Also, the website has been done by a chilean called Marcelo, a Spanish guy called Andres and an Italian one called Luca.
