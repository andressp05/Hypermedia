1: What is Paper House?

Answer

Paper House is a educational simulation of a bookshop created for the subject Hypermedia Applications of Politecnico di Milano university.

2: How can I filter the books?

Answer

You can filter them by literary genre and by topic. Also you can group them by literary genre and by topic. And in addition, we have a list of favourites and of bestsellers that you can see as well.
